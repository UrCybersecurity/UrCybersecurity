## About

Technology alone can't stop today's threats. UrCybersecurity arms employees and communities with the knowledge, habits, and tools to become the first line of defense — through hands-on cybersecurity and AI training that actually sticks.

Complex cybersecurity concepts, translated into clear, actionable language every employee can understand and apply immediately. Real-world simulations, not just slide decks.

Serving small and mid-size businesses, schools, and senior communities across the Raleigh-Durham Triangle, NC.
